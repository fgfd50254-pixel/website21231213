What is Touch Gras?

Touch Gras is a smart screen-time lock app that forces you to take a break from your phone. When you hit your limit, your apps lock — and the only way out is to step away and reset.

How It Works

Set Limits

Choose how long you can use your apps.

Auto Lock

Once time is up, your screen locks with a warning.

Break Required

No cheating — you must take a real break.

Reset & Return

Come back refreshed and unlock your apps.

Why Touch Gras?

Too much screen time? Touch Gras forces you to disconnect, recharge, and actually live life beyond your phone.
